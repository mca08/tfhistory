  • Characters Of Note

Optimus Prime

Leader of the Autobots and bearer of the Matrix of Leadership, Prime blames himself for bringing the war to Earth and has a long history with death.

Megatron

Former gladiator and leader of the Decepticons, Megatron rules with an iron fist, it is left ambigous if his future self is time traveling Decepticon Galvatron.

Starscream

The most treacherous Decepticon, Starscream has tried to betray Megatron countless times, he also has the highest kill count of any character via the Underbase Saga.

Galvatron

Time traveling from the year 2006, Galvatron's lackies Cyclonus and Scourge are ever loyal to him, despite his obvious insanity.

Scorponok

After Megatron was slain in a space brige explosion by Autobot medic Ratchet, Scorponok claimed leadership of the Decepticons, with both his brilliance with biogenetics, and cowardous in combat.

Grimlock

Me Grimlock very smart and humble yes. Me leader of Autobots because Prime no feel good. Me think I do good job as King.

Rodimus Prime

Leader of the Autobots succeeding Optimus Prime, he must adapt from a "turbo running young punk" into the leader everyone wants him to be.

Bumblebee

The little brother and kid appeal character of the show and comic, Bumblebee is notorious for getting into trouble with his human friends.

Thunderwing

The Matrix obsessed Decepticon general who ultimately attempted to steal the Matrix one last time, only to be eaten by Unicron.

Unicron

The dark god, the planet destroyer, the ultimate evil, Unicron's only weakness is the mystical Matrix of Leadership

Shockwave

Shockwave is the commander of the Decepticons who remained behind on Cybertron when the original Transformers landed on Earth, he runs on pure logic, which tells him he would be a better leader than Megatron.

Blaster

The Autobot communications officer, who briefly became a main character when he led a small team of Autobots in abandoning Grimlock's tyrannical rule of the Autobots.
